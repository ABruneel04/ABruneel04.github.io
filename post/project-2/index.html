Project 2: Quarterback Data Analysis

June 10, 2022

This project was a chance to work on web scraping and extracting meaning from data. I collected 5 seasons of fantasy football quarterback statistics from 2016-2020 and used visualizations to understand how different statistics interact with each other in ways that may not be obvious.

  • Assembled 7,200 data points of fantasy football quarterback statistics from an online database with BeautifulSoup and cleaned data to later be visualized
  • Engineered features inside of a pandas DataFrame, and used statistics to determine the highest scoring players over a five year period in both total points and points per game
  • Working to implement machine learning to predict a player’s rank in future seasons
